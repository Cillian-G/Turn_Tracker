Turn Tracker

Welcome to Turn Tracker, an online tool that helps you keep your board game night on track. Turn Tracker randomly selects a player to take the first turn, and will track whose turn it is throughout the game. Turn Tracker also has a handy timer function, so you can keep track of how long the current player's turn has lasted. At the end of your game, you'll get a tally of how much time each player spent taking their turns, so you can finally work out which players are dragging their feet and slowing down the game!


If players are already seated, selecting this setting will pick a random first player and will follow a turn order that matches the order that you're sitting in.

If players are not yet seated, or are not particularly attached to their current seats, selecting the random option will generate a completely random turn order.

Enter a name and color for each player. For the clockwise setting to work, make sure to enter your names in clockwise order around the table. Don't worry, it doesn't matter whose name is entered first!
