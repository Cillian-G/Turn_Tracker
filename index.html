Turn Tracker

PLACEHOLDER TEXT Welcome to Turn Tracker, an online tool that helps you keep track of whose turn it is when playing board games. Turn Tracker also has a handy timer function, so you can keep track of how long the current player's turn has lasted. At the end of your game, you'll get a tally of how much time each player took over the course of the game, so you can finally work out which players are dragging their feet and slowing down the game!

Clockwise or Random order

If players are already seated, selecting this setting will pick a random first player and will then allow you to play in the order in which you are already seated.

If players are not yet seated, or are not particularly attached to their current seats, selecting the random option will generate a completely random player order.

Enter player names

Enter a name and select a color for each player For the clockwise setting to work, make sure to enter your names in clockwise order around the table.
